Sapelo Island 1976

This aerial photomosaic of Blackbeard and Sapelo Islands show the island in 1976 compared to today patterns of the forest and maturity of the trees has signifcantly changed. Other changes visible from the air are the geomorphic changes of the beaches and Cabretta Inlet. The original aerial photographs that went into the making of this mosaic were captured by the Georgia Department of Transportation and are within the Aerial Photography Holdings at the Map and Government Information Library, UGA Libraries.
